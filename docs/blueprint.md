## Core Features:

- AI-Powered 3D Product Personalization Tool: A generative AI tool to transform user-uploaded photos into customizable 3D models, such as Litophane 'porta retrato abajur' or 1:10 sculptures in Funko, Cartoon, or Realistic styles. Includes text input for custom names ('DoubleName') and a real-time live preview of the personalized 3D model.
- Product Showcase & Discovery: An interactive homepage featuring a hero banner, institutional text, and browseable categories of personalized 3D printing products. Includes detailed product pages with customization options and displays of user-submitted images.
- Secure User Authentication & Profile: User registration, login, logout, and comprehensive management of user profiles, including personal information, addresses, and contact details stored in Firestore.
- E-commerce Cart & Secure Checkout: Functionality to add personalized 3D items to a shopping cart, adjust quantities, and proceed through a secure checkout process integrated with Stripe or Mercado Pago for payments.
- Order History & Status Tracking: A dedicated user dashboard where customers can conveniently view their past orders, review personalization details, and track the real-time status and shipping information for current orders.
- Product & Coupon Administration: An intuitive internal interface for platform administrators to manage product listings (add, edit, activate), inventory, and create/manage coupon codes for promotional campaigns within Firestore.
- Customer Review System: Enables users to submit star ratings and written reviews for their purchased personalized products, contributing to product validation and community engagement, stored in Firestore.

## Style Guidelines:

- Color scheme: Dark theme, embodying a futuristic and modern aesthetic. The primary color is a vibrant cyan (#5EE8E8), providing energy and clarity, conceptually derived from 'Turquesa'. The background is a deep, sophisticated charcoal with a subtle teal undertone (#1C2222). The accent color is a striking, slightly luminescent green (#3BCC9B), symbolizing creativity and used for calls to action, subtly referencing the 'Ouro' concept for contrast.
- Headline font: 'Space Grotesk' (sans-serif) for a modern, techy, and futuristic feel. Body font: 'Inter' (sans-serif) for clean readability and a neutral, objective aesthetic.
- Utilize sleek, minimalist geometric icons with sharp lines and potential subtle 3D-esque shading or metallic finishes, reinforcing the modern, futuristic, and industrial design theme of 3D printing.
- Employ a clean, modular, and content-first layout with generous negative space, optimized for full responsiveness. The design prioritizes a mobile-first approach, ensuring adaptability and clear hierarchy on all screen sizes using flexible grids.
- Implement subtle and fluid micro-interactions and transitions, such as smooth fades, glides, and vibrant hover effects. These animations should enhance user feedback and navigation without distracting from the personalized product experience, aligning with the modern UI.
